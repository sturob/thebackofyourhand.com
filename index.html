Re-Introducing London…

What is this exactly?

An interactive visualisation of Foursquare checkin history across London. Showing popular places to Eat Drink Shop Be Entertained

Why?

When exploring new cities I want more than just a map of highways and buildings. I want an insight into what people do and where they do it.

Why just London?

Because I live here (good for testing) and we're about the have the Olympics (good for tourism numbers). I plan to do more cities soon.
